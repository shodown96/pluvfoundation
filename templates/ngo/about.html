Welcome To PLUV

PLUV is not a Christian Organisation as many may want to say, PLUV is a Kingdom Promotion Organisation set out to fulfil or establish the will and agendas of the king of kings, which He has made us Kings and Priests to reign. We do not represent a religion or denomination or whatever you may call it, we represent a Kingdom from above. The King of Kings walked upon this earth and died for us that we may be reconciled back to the Kingdom of Heaven. The king has sent us as an organisation on a mission to represent the kingdom to the earth that many might be reconciled to this Kingdom. We are not ourselves; We are a representation of the Kingdom to deliver The Kings message .
